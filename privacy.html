Privacy Policy

We at SKAHM LTD respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information the Voice-to-Task Assistant app (“App”) collects, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

Information We Collect

When you authorize the App, we receive the minimum data needed to operate the App, such as your X username, posting permissions, and engagement metrics on your own posts. We do not collect private messages or unrelated personal data.

How We Use Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Automate posting to X on your behalf
  • Retrieve engagement metrics from your own posts
  • Improve the App’s performance and features

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. All data usage complies with the X Developer Agreement and applicable privacy laws.

Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Your Choices

You may revoke the App’s access to your X account at any time via your X settings. We will delete any stored data upon request.

Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@skahm-ltd.com.
